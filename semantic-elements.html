My Favorite Cities: A Semantic Webpage

Paris, France

Paris, France is the best city in the world. It's beautiful and easy to get around. My favorite thing to do is wander around Canal Saint-Martin.

San Francisco

San Francisco is my favorite American city. Even though it's only 7x7 miles, it can feel as cosmopolitan as cities like Vancouver and Berlin.

Conclusion

I've lucky enough to have lived in both places. I recommend people visit both. However, the cost of living makes San Francisco less appealing. If you can speak French, you'll probably prefer living in Paris.
